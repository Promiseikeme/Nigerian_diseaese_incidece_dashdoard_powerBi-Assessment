**NIGERIAN DISEASE SURVEILLIANCE ANALYSIS**

**Project Overview**
This project analyzes disease incidence and mortality trends across Nigeria States between 2010 and 2018. The dashboard focuses on major disease such as malaria, cholera, Meninggitis and others, providing insights on how disease cases and deaths vary by state, settlement type, age group and reporting year. 

**Objective**The goal is to understand disease pattern by transforming raw health data into meaninful insights that can support public health monitoring and decision making.

**Problem Statement** Public health authorities need clear data driven insights to identify high-incidence states, mornitor mortality trends, compare disease spread across settlements and detect high-risk years.

**Dataset DescriptionThe** dataset used contains health surveilliance record with the following field = ID, Gender, State, Settlement, Date of birth, Report date, Age, Age group, Disease & Health Status.


**Key performance indicators (KPIs)**  from the  analysis, Total cases: 2246, total death: 1158,Death rate: 0.52, Cholera Death 130. these provide a quick overview of disease burden and mortality in the dataset.

**Insights:** State_level disease incidence compares malaria and cholera across Nigerian States, highlighting which states have higher disease occurrence. Disease distribution by settlement compares urban and Rural area showing a nearly balanced distribution between the two.The mortality rate between 2010 and 2018 highlights how disease impact has changed overtime. The line chart shows how meningitis cases fluctuate across reporting years, identifing peaks & decline in disease occurrence.

**Tools & Technologies used** PowerBi,Dax, Data cleaning & tranformation, Data Visualization Techniques.

**Skills Demostrated** Data analysis & interpretation, Dashboard design, data storytelling, public health data analytics PowerBi development.
